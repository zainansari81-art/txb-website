Process

Seven stages. No surprises.

Every TXB project, from a single-day shoot to a multi-month campaign, follows the same structured workflow. Clients know what happens next, who is responsible, and when each milestone lands.

01
Discovery
We start with your objectives: who the work is for, what it needs to achieve, and the constraints around budget, timeline, and approvals. This stage defines scope before any creative work begins.
Project brief Scope document Estimate
02
Creative Direction
Concepts, references, and tone are developed and presented for approval. For scripted work this includes the core message architecture and visual treatment.
Concept deck Treatment Visual references
03
Pre-Production
Scripts are finalized, storyboards drawn, locations scouted, talent and crew booked, and the shoot schedule locked. Everything is confirmed in writing before cameras roll.
Final script Storyboard Shoot schedule
04
Production
Filming and photography, whether studio or location, single or multi-camera. A producer manages the day so the schedule holds and every planned shot is captured.
Footage Photography Audio
05
Post-Production
Edit, color grade, sound design, motion graphics, and any required subtitling or localization. Work-in-progress cuts are shared at defined checkpoints.
First cut Graded master Graphics
06
Review
Structured feedback rounds with consolidated client notes. Revisions are tracked against the agreed scope so the review stays efficient and predictable.
Review cuts Revision log
07
Final Delivery
Approved masters delivered in every required format, including web, social, broadcast, and corporate, with organized file naming and archive-ready project files.
Final masters Format exports Archive

Start at stage one.

Every project begins with a discovery conversation, with no commitment required.
