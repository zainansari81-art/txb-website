About Us

A creative production company built around execution.

TXB (SMC-PRIVATE) LIMITED exists to give businesses, organizations, and institutions a production partner they can rely on for creative quality, for process, and for delivery.

Who we are

Creative work, delivered with discipline.

TXB is a Karachi-based creative media company specializing in video production, commercial production, photography, content creation, and post-production. We work with brands, corporations, public-sector organizations, and institutions that need professional visual communication, produced to standard and on schedule.

Our work is built on visual storytelling: understanding what a message needs to achieve, then shaping concept, script, cinematography, and edit around that goal. Every project runs through a structured production workflow, from discovery and creative direction through pre-production, filming, post-production, review, and final delivery, so quality is a repeatable outcome and not an accident.

We measure ourselves on execution and on relationships. Most of our engagements are long-term: clients who return for their next campaign, their next event, their next quarter of content, because the work was good and the process was easy.

How we operate

Four commitments on every project

01
High-quality execution
Craft at every stage: cinematography, sound, edit, and grade. We hold the same standard on a thirty-second reel as on a broadcast commercial.
02
Structured workflows
Every project follows a defined seven-stage process with documented scope, approvals, and review points. Clients always know where their project stands.
03
Reliable delivery
Timelines are set at kickoff and kept. Deliverables arrive in the formats agreed, whether web, social, broadcast, or corporate, ready to publish.
04
Long-term relationships
We build for repeat work: clear communication, honest estimates, and production support that scales with a client's needs over time.
